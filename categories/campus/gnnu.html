Gannan Normal University

Located in the central city of Ganzhou, GNU was founded in 1958. It is one of the two provincial normal universities in Jiangxi province, owning 10 disciplines — Law, Economics, History, Literature, Science, Engineering, Agriculture, Education, Management and Arts, with 20 academic faculties, 67 bachelor’s degree programs, and 66 master’s degree programs. There are over 20,000 students and about 1300 faculty. GNU occupies an area of 2100 mu, and has good educational resources and conditions.
